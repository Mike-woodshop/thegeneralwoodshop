Custom Orders

We do custom work. If you've got a specific wood, size, or design in mind, send us a message and we'll figure it out together. Personalized wood burning for gifts and special occasions is one of our favorite things to do — names, dates, portraits, logos.

What to Include

For custom orders, it helps if you can tell us:

  • What type of product you're interested in
  • Approximate size if you have a preference
  • Wood species or color preference
  • Any personalization (names, dates, images)
  • When you need it by

Turnaround

Most pieces take 1–3 weeks depending on complexity and what's already in progress. Custom end-grain cutting boards and wood-burned portraits can take longer. We'll give you an honest timeline upfront.
